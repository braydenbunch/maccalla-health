Quick questions

Common Medicare questions.

When should I start thinking about Medicare?

Many people start before turning 65 so there is time to understand enrollment windows, plan types, and prescription coverage.

Can you help if I am already on Medicare?

Yes. The agency can help review whether your current plan still fits your doctors, prescriptions, and budget.

Can my family join the appointment?

Yes. If a spouse, child, caregiver, or parent helps with decisions, they can be part of the conversation.

Do you explain plans in normal language?

Yes. The point is to make the choices easier to understand without pressure or confusing terms.
