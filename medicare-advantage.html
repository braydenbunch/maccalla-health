What Part C means

Medicare Advantage, also called Part C, is offered by private insurance companies approved by Medicare. These plans replace how you receive Original Medicare benefits.

What plans may include

Plans can include medical coverage and may include prescription, dental, vision, hearing, fitness, transportation, or other extra benefits depending on the plan and area.

What to compare

Provider networks, prescriptions, premiums, copays, out-of-pocket maximums, referrals, and extra benefits all matter when comparing plans.
