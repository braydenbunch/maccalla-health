Part A hospital insurance

Part A helps cover inpatient hospital care, skilled nursing facility care after a qualifying hospital stay, hospice, and limited home health care.

Part B medical insurance

Part B helps cover doctor visits, outpatient care, preventive services, lab work, imaging, and certain medical equipment.

Coverage gaps to understand

Original Medicare does not include everything. Many people review prescription drug coverage, dental, vision, hearing, travel coverage, and protection from high out-of-pocket costs.
