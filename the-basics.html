The four main parts

A, B, C, and D — decoded

A

Hospital Insurance

Covers inpatient hospital care, skilled nursing stays, hospice, and limited home health services. Most people don't pay a premium for Part A.

B

Medical Insurance

Covers outpatient care like doctor visits, preventive services, lab work, and medical equipment. There is a monthly premium for Part B.

C

Medicare Advantage

Private plans that include everything from Parts A and B — often adding drug coverage and extras like dental, vision, or hearing.

D

Prescription Drugs

Helps pay for medications. Offered by private insurers, either standalone or included in a Medicare Advantage plan.
